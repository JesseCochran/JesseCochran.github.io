About Me

Welcome to my professional journey.

I am Jesse Cochran, a dedicated software engineering student based in Melbourne, Australia. With a profound passion for programming and a commitment to excellence, my journey in the world of technology is marked by continuous learning and impactful projects.

As a first-year student at RMIT Australia, I have delved into the intricacies of discrete math, honed my Java skills in bootcamps, and mastered advanced techniques in C++. This academic foundation forms the backbone of my technical expertise. Additionally, I completed an AI course where I explored the fascinating realm of artificial intelligence using Python. The course delved into advanced concepts, with a particular focus on neural networks. This experience has enriched my understanding of machine learning and its practical applications, further expanding my skill set in the ever-evolving field of technology.

My entrance into the professional realm includes a rich history in game development using Unity and C# from 2016 to 2021. In recent times, I have embraced the challenges of Android app development, creating applications like a calculator, a number converter, and a music player.

The fusion of technology and environmental responsibility is exemplified in my project involving a climate change website. This endeavor reflects my belief in using technology for positive change and making a meaningful impact.

Connect with me on LinkedIn and GitHub to stay updated on my latest projects and contributions. I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and look forward to connecting with fellow enthusiasts and professionals.
